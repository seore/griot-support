📖 Getting Started

1. Grant Microphone Permission

  1. Tap the microphone button on the main screen
  2. When prompted, tap "Allow" to grant microphone access
  3. This permission is required for voice translation to work

2. Select Your Languages

  1. Tap on the source language (left side) to choose what language you'll speak
  2. Tap on the target language (right side) to choose the translation language
  3. Use the ⇄ button to quickly swap languages

3. Start Translating

  1. Tap the large circular microphone button
  2. Speak clearly in your selected language
  3. Wait for the translation to appear
  4. The translation will automatically be spoken aloud

✨ Features

💬 Conversation Mode

For real-time back-and-forth conversations:

  1. Tap the 💬 Conversation Mode button
  2. Speak your phrase and wait for translation
  3. Hand the phone to the other person
  4. They can tap the mic and respond in their language
  5. You can switch between continous and manual for translations
  6. The conversation is saved in the chat history

🔍 Auto-Detect Language

Don't know what language someone is speaking?

  1. Tap the source language (left side)
  2. Select 🔍 Auto-Detect from the menu
  3. GRIOT will automatically identify the spoken language
  4. Perfect for multi-language environments

📦 Offline Mode

Common phrases work without internet:

  • Greetings (Hello, Good morning, etc.)
  • Basic phrases (Thank you, Please, Yes, No)
  • Essential questions (How are you?, How much?)
  • Emergency phrases (I need help, Where is...?)

Look for the 📦 Works Offline badge on translations.

🔧 Troubleshooting

❓ App isn't hearing me / Microphone not working

Solution:

  1. Go to Settings → GRIOT → Microphone
  2. Make sure it's set to Allow
  3. Restart the app
  4. Ensure your device microphone isn't blocked or damaged

❓ Translation isn't appearing

Solution:

  • Check your internet connection (required for most translations)
  • Try offline mode for common phrases
  • Speak more clearly and slowly
  • Reduce background noise
  • Ensure the correct languages are selected

❓ Audio translation not playing

Solution:

  • Check your device volume
  • Make sure Silent Mode is off
  • Try tapping the 🔊 Play Again button
  • Restart the app

❓ Translation quality is poor

Solution:

  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Minimize background noise
  • Use simple, clear sentences

❓ App is slow or freezing

Solution:

  1. Close other running apps
  2. Restart GRIOT
  3. Restart your device
  4. Check for app updates in the App Store

💡 Tips for Best Results

🎤 Speaking Tips:
  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Hold the phone 6-8 inches from your mouth
  • Minimize background noise
  • Use complete sentences when possible
📱 Device Tips:
  • Keep your app updated to the latest version
  • Ensure stable Wi-Fi or cellular connection
  • Close unnecessary background apps
  • Restart your device periodically
🌍 Translation Tips:
  • Simple, clear sentences translate better
  • Avoid slang or very technical terms
  • For conversations, let each person finish speaking
  • Verify important translations with context

📱 System Requirements

  • iOS: iOS 15.0 or later
  • Device: iPhone 8 or newer
  • Internet: Required for most translations (offline mode for select phrases)
  • Permissions: Microphone access required
  • Storage: Approximately 50 MB
